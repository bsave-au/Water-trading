Submit a New Listing

How it works This free service helps Queensland water entitlement and allocation holders connect with potential buyers and sellers. Listings are reviewed before publication. When a user expands a listing, the contact details of the listing holder are displayed so they can reach out directly. All trades must be approved by the Department of Local Government, Water and Volunteers and recorded in the water allocations register. Seek independent legal and financial advice before trading.

Relationship with the Queensland Government

The Notice Board is a free service provided by the Department of Local Government, Water and Volunteers to help match buyers and sellers of water entitlements and allocations. The Queensland Government is not a party to any trade and does not endorse or recommend any listing.

Contact Information

By posting a listing, you consent to your name, phone number and email address being displayed publicly when a user expands your listing. You agree to immediately notify the Department if your details change or your listing is no longer current, so it can be updated or removed.

Accuracy

You must provide full and accurate information. The Queensland Government does not warrant the accuracy or currency of listings. Users rely on information at their own risk and should conduct their own due diligence.

Water Trading Rules

Trading is governed by the Water Act 2000 (Qld) and applicable water plan trading rules. All transfers must be approved by the Department and recorded in the water allocations register. Seek independent legal, financial and tax advice before entering into any trade.

Privacy

Personal information is managed in accordance with the Information Privacy Act 2009 (Qld). Contact: WaterHub@dlgwv.qld.gov.au.

Liability

To the extent permitted by law, the Queensland Government is not liable for any loss arising from use of the Notice Board.
